Instructions
Drop any transparent-background PNG to generate an AoM Retold-style aura icon with customisable glow, color, and placement across four presets (Hero, Myth, Legend, Villain). Adjust all parameters live and download the result at 512, 256, or 128 px.
For best results, use a source image with the unit centered on a transparent background, ideally around 512×512 px. The zoom and offset controls let you fine-tune the unit's placement within the icon. The glow parameters allow you to achieve the desired intensity and spread of the aura effect.
